NEA & War Powers — Resolution Templates (2027)

Keep privileged floor paths clean. Run these as standalones while the appropriations riders and Minibus-1 safeguards do their work.

National Emergencies Act — Termination (Joint Resolution)

JOINT RESOLUTION
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled,
That the national emergency declared on [DATE] by Proclamation [NUMBER] is hereby terminated.

War Powers Resolution — Removal (Concurrent Resolution, House Vehicle)

CONCURRENT RESOLUTION
Resolved by the House of Representatives (the Senate concurring),
That, pursuant to section 5(c) of the War Powers Resolution (50 U.S.C. 1544(c)), the President shall remove United States Armed Forces from hostilities in [THEATER] not later than [XX] days after the adoption of this resolution, unless Congress enacts a specific authorization.

War Powers — Removal (Joint Resolution to Present)

JOINT RESOLUTION
Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled,
SECTION 1. Short title; findings; purpose. [Optional]
SEC. 2. Removal from hostilities. The President shall remove United States Armed Forces from hostilities in [THEATER] not later than [XX] days after the enactment of this joint resolution, unless Congress enacts a specific authorization.
