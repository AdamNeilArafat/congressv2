CRA Disapproval — Resolution Templates (2027)

Use one resolution per rule. Keep them clean. Move the easiest wins first to build momentum.

Clock checklist

  • Confirm the rule’s submission to Congress and the Government Accountability Office.
  • Verify it is within the CRA review window (including look-back).
  • Lock the exact FR citation and date before filing.

CRA Joint Resolution Template

JOINT RESOLUTION
Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by [Agency] relating to “[Rule Title]”.

Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled,
That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the [Agency], relating to “[Rule Title]”, and such rule shall have no force or effect.
[Optional section: rule citation details — Federal Register volume, page, date; docket number.]

Notes

  • Do not bundle multiple rules. File individually.
  • Pair public comms with your dashboard narrative from Minibus-1 for clarity.
