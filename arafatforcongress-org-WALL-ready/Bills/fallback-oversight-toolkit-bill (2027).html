Fallback Oversight Toolkit Bill (2027)

When Senate/conference kills the core package, keep leverage: standing House legal authority, rapid GAO audits, OMB dashboard duties, and ready-to-use riders and privileged resolutions (as companions).

House Counsel Authority 60-Day GAO Audits OMB Dashboard Model Riders/Directives Fast Court Lane

TITLE I — PURPOSE & FINDINGS

Purpose. Provide an immediate oversight backstop if core safeguards fail in the Senate or in conference; coordinate House litigation, audits, and transparency to freeze unlawful implementation.

Project history context: This captures your “If pieces die…” plan: committee report directives, Holman/surgical riders, NEA/WPR/CRA tools, and litigation readiness.

TITLE II — HOUSE LITIGATION AUTHORITY

Standing authorization for the Office of General Counsel to seek TROs/preliminary injunctions/declaratory relief to enforce publication/authentication and use-of-funds conditions during covered periods; amicus authority; reporting to the House.

TITLE III — OMB DASHBOARD DUTY

Mandate continuous, public, machine-readable dashboard listing challenged actions, compliance IDs, certifications, court status, and agency remediation steps.

TITLE IV — GAO RAPID AUDITS

GAO to audit and report within 60 days after each covered period ends, plus interim “flash” reports if material risks appear (publication gaps, misuse of seals, routing bypasses).

TITLE V — JUDICIAL REVIEW

Three-judge district court; direct appeal; no automatic stay; standard injunctive criteria.

TITLE VI — COMPANION TEMPLATES (NON-STATUTORY)

  • Committee Report Directives: OFR/GPO/OMB/OPM + GSA/CIO/IG language; tie to approps lines.
  • Surgical Riders & Holman: Model text to fence publication/authentication and target desks (e.g., OIRA intake) when needed.
  • NEA/WPR Resolutions: Privileged vote templates to terminate emergencies or remove forces.
  • CRA Disapprovals: One-rule-per-resolution templates to void impeached-period rules finalized late in the window.

Publish these as separate, linked templates so they can move in their proper procedural lanes.

TITLE VII — SUNSET & SEVERABILITY

Four-year sunset aligned with the other safeguards; severable; effective on enactment.
